# Roadmap

This roadmap is intentionally short and near-term.

## Retrieval Quality

- improve chunk ranking beyond simple lexical heuristics
- better prefer findings and conclusion-bearing passages
- reduce methodological/background chunks in top-ranked sets

## Claim Quality

- improve extraction consistency across study types
- continue tightening span faithfulness
- reduce unsupported specificity in claim text

## Evaluation

- strengthen automated claim evaluation prompts and summaries
- add better benchmark-style test cases for known questions
- make weak claims easier to diagnose quickly

## Brief Synthesis

- further prioritize stronger, more faithful claims
- improve handling of conflicting findings
- improve caveat and uncertainty language

## Open Source Readiness

- add curated screenshots
- add example runs and reproducible test questions
- improve issue templates and contributor guidance over time
